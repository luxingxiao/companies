The engineering team builds and maintains the agency's capability infrastructure. The Skills Architect creates skills, subagents, and MCP servers. The Workflow Designer wires commands, hooks, and processes. The Quality Auditor reviews everything against best practices. Together they ensure the agency's tools are well-built, well-connected, and well-tested.
